Our Services

CV ANALYSIS AND JOB DESCRIPTION MATCHING

The CV analysis and job description matching feature streamlines the recruitment process by thoroughly analyzing candidate CVs, identifying key information and evaluating their alignment with job requirements. It intelligently matches keywords and assesses skill proficiency, automatically ranking candidates for suitability.

ACADEMIC TRANSCRIPT ANALYSIS

The Academic Transcript Skills Identification feature is a tool that scans academic transcripts to extract and categorize skills and competencies acquired during a candidate's education. It offers a visually engaging representation of the candidate's skills, maps skills to job roles, and highlights any skill gaps, aiding recruiters in recognizing a candidate's potential skill areas.

PROFESSIONAL SKILLS ANALYSIS

This service evaluates information from platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn, along with referee feedbacks, to gain a comprehensive understanding of candidates' skills and proficiencies.

PERSONALITY ASSESSMENT

The Personality Assessment feature is used to assess the suitability of the candidate for the job. This is done by evaluating a candidate's Big Five personality traits and then analyzing how well these traits align with the personality traits needed for the specific job role they are applying for.
